Descriptive Statistics

What is descriptive statistics, and what does it help us do?

Stats tap to reveal ↓
Answer

Descriptive statistics summarises and describes the main features of a dataset — without drawing conclusions beyond the data itself.

Central tendency
Mean, Median, Mode
Spread / dispersion
Range, Variance, Std Dev
Shape
Skewness, Kurtosis
Position
Percentiles, Quartiles
